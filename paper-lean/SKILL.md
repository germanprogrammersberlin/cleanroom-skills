# Paper Lean

Remove redundancies and shorten the paper systematically.

## What to Do

- Same statement in Introduction AND Discussion? Shorten or remove one
- Paragraph repeats what the figure caption already says? Shorten the paragraph
- Section exceeds length target? Identify and cut unnecessary sentences
- Word limit exceeded? Prioritize cutting: repetitions > details > examples

## What NOT to Do

- Do not change content (no new arguments, no new data)
- Do not remove references (that is a separate skill)
- Do not change writing style (that is a separate skill)

## After Every Change

Check: Did I accidentally delete a reference, a value, or a figure reference? If yes, restore immediately.
