# Writing Quality Review

Review writing quality systematically, section by section.

## Check per Section

1. **Tense consistency** — Does the paragraph switch between past and present?
2. **which vs. that** — Restrictive (that) vs. non-restrictive (which) correct?
3. **Sentence length** — Sentences over ~30 words?
4. **Clarity** — Is every sentence understandable on first reading?
5. **Passive voice** — Excessive passive usage?
6. **Precision** — Vague wording ("some", "various", "significant" without data)?

## How to Work

1. Read one section
2. Check all 6 criteria
3. Report each problem with exact location (section, paragraph, sentence)
4. Next section

Do NOT review the whole paper at once — section by section.

## Self-Verification

Re-read your findings. Is each one justified or just a matter of taste? Remove taste-based findings. Keep only real problems.
