# Structure Review

Review the structural completeness of a scientific paper.

## Title

- Concise and attractive?
- Highlights the main contribution?

## Abstract

- Complete (background, method, result, significance)?
- Under word limit?
- Matches the actual paper content?

## Introduction (6 criteria)

1. Broad topic and its importance presented?
2. Specific problem clearly stated?
3. Challenges explained?
4. Solution approach outlined?
5. Reading guide (what comes in which section)?
6. Main contributions listed?

## Section Organization

- Logical flow: Introduction → Methods → Results → Discussion → Conclusion?
- Each section has sufficient content?
- Transitions between sections?

## Output

For each problem found, report:
- Where: exact section and paragraph
- What: precisely what is missing or wrong
- Severity: critical / major / minor
